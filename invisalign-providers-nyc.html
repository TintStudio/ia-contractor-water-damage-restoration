Frequently Asked Questions

What is Invisalign?
Invisalign is a type of orthodontic treatment that uses clear aligners to straighten teeth.
How do I find an Invisalign provider in NYC?
You can find an Invisalign provider in NYC by searching online directories, asking your dentist for a referral, or checking the Invisalign website for a list of certified providers.
What factors should I consider when choosing an Invisalign provider in NYC?
Consider factors such as the providers experience, qualifications, patient reviews, the technology they use, and the overall cost of treatment.
What is the average cost of Invisalign treatment in NYC?
The average cost of Invisalign treatment in NYC can vary depending on the complexity of the case, but it typically ranges from $3,000 to $8,000.
How long does Invisalign treatment typically take?
The length of Invisalign treatment varies depending on individual needs, but it typically takes between 12 to 18 months.
